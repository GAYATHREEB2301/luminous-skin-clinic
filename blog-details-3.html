Selecting the Ideal Mineral Sunscreen

Sun protection is the non-negotiable cornerstone of any effective skincare routine. While chemical filters have dominated the market for years, mineral sunscreens (also known as physical blockers) are making a major comeback at Luminous Skin Clinic.

The Mineral Advantage

Based on Zinc Oxide or Titanium Dioxide, mineral sunscreens sit on top of the skin and reflect UV rays like a mirror. They are inherently photostable and start working the moment they are appliedâ€”no 20-minute waiting period required.

Perfect for Sensitive Skin

Because mineral filters don't penetrate the skin or convert UV to heat with the same intensity as chemical filters, they are significantly less likely to cause irritation or spark a rosacea flare-up.
