Chemical Peels: Demystifying the Process

Chemical peels are one of the most misunderstood treatments in dermatology. Often associated with intense redness and "shedding like a snake," modern chemical resurfacing is actually a controlled and sophisticated way to reveal fresher, more even skin.

What Actually Happens?

A specialized acid solution is applied to the skin, which lowers the pH of the epidermal layers. This weakens the bonds between dead skin cells, encouraging them to lift away and stimulating the collagen-production centers in the dermis below.

The Depth of Treatment

At Luminous Skin Clinic, we offer three tiers of peeling: Superficial (Lunchtime Peels), Medium-depth (for pigmentation and fine lines), and Deep (for significant textural correction).
