Maintaining Your Glow Throughout Winter

As the temperature drops and the air becomes drier, your skin's natural moisture barrier faces its toughest challenge of the year. The transition from biting cold winds outdoors to overheated indoor spaces can leave your complexion feeling tight, flaky, and dull.

1. Switch to a Creamy Cleanser

Gel or foam cleansers that worked perfectly in the humid summer months might be too stripping now. A cream-based cleanser removes impurities while depositing essential lipids back into the skin.

2. Layer Your Hydration

Don't rely on just one heavy cream. Start with a hydrating serum containing Hyaluronic Acid or Glycerin, and then seal that moisture in with a nourishing moisturizer rich in ceramides.
